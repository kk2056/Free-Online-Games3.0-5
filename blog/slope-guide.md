Gameplay Tips: The Art of the Flip
The core mechanic of Moto X3M isn't speed; it's the time reduction bonus.

Strategy: To get 3 stars, you must flip whenever you have enough airtime. Even a small jump can be enough for a quick backflip if your bike is upgraded or you have high momentum.
Advanced Level Strategies
Rear Wheel Landings: When falling from a great height, try to land on your rear wheel. This prevents your rider's head from hitting the ground if the terrain is uneven and allows for faster acceleration upon landing.
